教育理念

Our Educational Philosophy

语用学（Pragmatics）告诉我们，语言的意义不仅在于词汇和语法，更在于它被使用的情境。我们相信，当学习者真正需要表达时，语言习得的效率将远超机械练习。

Pragmatics teaches us that the meaning of language lies not only in vocabulary and grammar, but in the context of its use. We believe that when learners genuinely need to express themselves, language acquisition becomes far more effective than mechanical practice.

01

情境优先

Context First

语言学习应当从具体的使用场景出发，而非孤立的词汇表

Language learning should begin with concrete usage scenarios, not isolated vocabulary lists

02

意义驱动

Meaning-Driven

当学习者理解「为什么要说」，「怎么说」便水到渠成

When learners understand "why to say it", "how to say it" follows naturally

03

从容习得

Unhurried Acquisition

尊重每位学习者的节奏，让语言能力在无压力中自然生长

Respecting each learner's pace, allowing language skills to grow naturally without pressure
